What is VARSE?

App for Android that allows to record HR (Heart Rate) signals acquired with a chest strap connected by Bluetooth to the mobile device, and performs HRV (Heart Rate Variability) analysis in real time.

HR data can be recorded and labelled while individuals are performing differente mental and/or physical activities of limited duration.
